# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales performance and customer purchasing behavior using Tableau dashboards to uncover trends, business KPIs, and actionable business insights.

The dashboard was built using Tableau Public and focuses on:

* Sales performance tracking
* Customer demographic analysis
* Product category analysis
* Revenue trends over time
* Interactive filtering and dashboard design

---

# Tools Used

* Tableau Public
* CSV Dataset
* Data Visualization
* Dashboard Design
* Business Intelligence Reporting

---

# Dataset

Dataset used:

Retail Sales Dataset from Kaggle

The dataset includes:

* Transaction data
* Customer demographics
* Product categories
* Revenue information
* Purchase dates

---

# Dashboard Features

## KPI Cards

* Total Revenue
* Estimated Profit
* Profit Margin
* Total Transactions
* Average Order Value

## Visualizations

### Sales Trend Over Time

Tracks monthly revenue performance and sales growth trends.

### Sales by Product Category

Identifies top-performing product categories.

### Customer Demographic Analysis

Analyzes purchasing behavior across customer demographics.

### Sales by Age Group

Shows which customer age groups generate the most revenue.

### Revenue by Category

Highlights product categories contributing the highest revenue.

---

# Key Insights

* Certain product categories generated significantly higher revenue than others.
* Revenue trends showed consistent sales fluctuations throughout the year.
* Customer purchasing behavior varied across different age groups.
* A small number of product categories accounted for a large percentage of total revenue.
* Average order value remained relatively stable across transactions.

---

# Business Recommendations

* Increase marketing investment toward high-performing product categories.
* Develop targeted campaigns for high-value customer demographics.
* Monitor seasonal sales trends for inventory planning.
* Use customer demographic insights to improve promotional strategies.
* Focus retention efforts on repeat purchasing behavior.
