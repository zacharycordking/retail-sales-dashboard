# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales performance and customer purchasing behavior using Tableau dashboards to uncover trends, business KPIs, and actionable business insights.

The dashboard was built using Tableau Public and focuses on:

* Sales performance tracking
* Customer demographic analysis
* Product category analysis
* Revenue trends over time
* Interactive filtering and dashboard design

---

# Tools Used

* Tableau Public
* CSV Dataset
* Data Visualization
* Dashboard Design
* Business Intelligence Reporting

---

# Dataset

Dataset used:

Retail Sales Dataset from Kaggle

The dataset includes:

* Transaction data
* Customer demographics
* Product categories
* Revenue information
* Purchase dates

---

# Dashboard Features

## KPI Cards

* Total Transactions
* Average Order Value
* Estimated Profit
* Total Revenue
* Profit Margin

## Visualizations

### Monthly Sales Trend

Tracks monthly sales performance and revenue fluctuations throughout the year.

### Revenue by Product Category

Identifies the highest-performing product categories based on total revenue.

### Customer Demographics

Compares purchasing behavior between male and female customers.

### Sales by Age Group

Analyzes revenue generated across different customer age segments.

### Revenue Heatmap by Product Category

Provides a detailed comparison of purchasing behavior across age groups and product categories using a heatmap visualization.

---

# Key Insights

* Electronics generated the highest overall revenue among product categories.
* Customers aged 25-54 accounted for the majority of total sales revenue.
* Female customers generated slightly higher total revenue than male customers.
* Monthly sales trends showed strong peaks during May and October.
* Customers aged 25-34 spent the most on Clothing products.
* Electronics performed strongly among customers aged 35 and older.

---

# Business Recommendations

* Increase marketing investment toward high-performing product categories.
* Develop targeted campaigns for high-value customer demographics.
* Monitor seasonal sales trends for inventory planning.
* Use customer demographic insights to improve promotional strategies.
* Focus retention efforts on repeat purchasing behavior.
